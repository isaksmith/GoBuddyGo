Future implementations

1. Adaptive difficulty that adjusts speed, steering sensitivity, and obstacle density based on player performance.
2. Progression system with unlockable cars, paint jobs, and badges.
3. Daily/weekly challenges to increase replayability.
4. Parent-facing dashboard with practice time, focus streaks, and milestone summaries.
5. Accessibility presets (larger controls, slower acceleration curves, reduced reaction mode).
6. Ghost racing against your best lap.
7. AR and 3D optimization: model caching, quality tiers by device, and usage analytics.
8. Reliability upgrades: error boundaries, better recovery UX for model/network failures.
9. Performance budgets in CI for startup time, FPS, and memory.
10. Feature flags + staged release channels for safer rollouts.
11. End-to-end smoke tests for critical flows (race start, finish, summary, parent mode).
12. Visual regression testing and telemetry dashboards (crash-free sessions, completion rate, asset load failures).
